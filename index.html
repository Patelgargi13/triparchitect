📖 Step-by-Step

How to Use It

Get your perfect trip plan in under 2 minutes

01

Enter Your Trip Details

Go to Plan My Trip, type your destination, pick travel dates, budget level and your trip style — adventure, culture, romance, food or relaxation.

02

AI Builds Your Perfect Plan

Our Gemini AI crafts a complete day-by-day itinerary — real place names, morning/afternoon/evening activities, local tips, weather, maps, phrasebook and more.

03

Explore All 9 Tabs

Browse your itinerary, live weather forecast, interactive map, country facts, local phrases, packing checklist, emergency numbers, budget breakdown and local festivals.

04

Download & Travel!

Download your itinerary as a .txt file or print as PDF. Screenshot the phrasebook for offline use. Pack your bags — your perfect trip is ready!
